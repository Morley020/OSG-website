About the Founder

Brigadier General Oliver Mubita (Ret.)

A distinguished Army General, visionary strategist, and internationally recognised defence and security expert with vast experience in Visionary and Transformational Leadership — at home and abroad.

Gen. Ollie is not only a practitioner but brings to the fore a hybrid of academic and practical experience. He has served with the United Nations, African Union, Regional Mechanisms and Economic Communities like the Southern African Development Community (SADC) — including Civil Society and the corporate world — bridging the gap between Public and Private sectors.

Academically, Gen. Mubita holds a Master of Military Arts and Sciences in Strategic Studies from the prestigious US Army War College (in collaboration with Central Michigan University), a Bachelor's in Business Administration in Project Management, and is currently pursuing an MBA in Leadership and Strategy. He was also trained at the Irish Military College, the Canadian Peace Support Training Centre, and the African Union Commission.

Through Ollie's Strategic Group, he employs a number of tested change models whilst cultivating and promoting operational resilience and recovery — incorporating the interplay between systems to achieve desired effects. It is an opportunity of renewal, affording a chance to build and enhance systems. Let us do it together!

Career & Education

Credentials & Achievements

A career defined by excellence, international exposure, and unwavering commitment to peace, strategy, and leadership.

Key Career Milestones

2023 – Present

Founder, Ollie's Strategic Group

Private strategic practice in Defence, Security, Corporate Governance, Leadership Mentoring & Coaching.

2021 – 2022

Commandant — Defence Services Command & Staff College

Principal adviser to MoD on military education. Oversaw 200+ officers, staff and learners. Led international cooperation with multiple partners.

2015 – 2019

Senior Officer / Deputy Commandant — SADC RPTC, Harare

Principal adviser to the SADC Organ for Politics, Defence and Security. Key contributor to SADC Strategic Plans and Force operational capability.

2019 – 2021

Military Assistant to Zambia Army Commander

Personal strategic staff appointment. Technical adviser at the apex of Zambia's military leadership.

2007 – 2008

Chief Operations Officer — UN Mission in Sudan (UNMIS)

Operations planning in the volatile Abeyi area. Liaison with UN agencies. Security advice to the Joint Military Committee.

2004 – 2005

Liaison Officer / DDR Coordinator — UN Mission in DRC (MONUC)

Coordination of Disarmament, Demobilisation and Reintegration in Ituri province. Liaison with armed groups for ceasefire implementation.
